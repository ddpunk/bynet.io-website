Meet Protocol Layers

Matchmaking Engine

Matchmaking is a service that executes transactions by matching appropriate orders from the order book. Matchmaker is an algorithmic program that performs swaps while getting the difference between the bid and ask prices. It can decide on the commission they are aiming for, with diverse strategies dependent on a specific market.

Stake Pool Operators are a crucial part of the Cardano network, and they will be incentivized to perform swaps as matchmakers with various options of rewarding their delegators.

How it works

From order to settlement, on-chain

  1. 01

    Place a Liquidity Order

    Providers post orders to the on-chain order book, setting size and a fee.

  2. 02

    Matchmaking

    The engine finds appropriate orders and prepares a swap across the book.

  3. 03

    Swap executes

    Matchmakers execute the swap, capturing the bid-ask spread as commission.

  4. 04

    Fees & rewards

    Fees flow to providers; Stake Pool Operators are rewarded for matchmaking.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Bynet Protocol?

Bynet Protocol is a decentralized trading protocol based on the Order Book pattern. It provides a fundamental on-chain order book and an infrastructure layer that lets projects build DeFi products on top of it on Cardano.

What is the on-chain Order Book?

It’s a fully on-chain order book that DApps can develop on top of, using shared liquidity instead of bootstrapping their own market from scratch.

How does the Matchmaking Engine work?

Matchmaking is an algorithmic service that matches appropriate orders and performs swaps, capturing the difference between bid and ask prices. Matchmakers can choose their commission and strategy per market.

Who can build on Bynet Protocol?

Any project can use the shared liquidity and development layer to launch DeFi products quickly - for example projects like BynetDEX.

What is a Liquidity Order?

A Liquidity Order can increase in size with a fee set by the provider, be partially fulfillable, and have its distribution adjusted to price dynamics - enabling greater returns as more swaps are performed.
